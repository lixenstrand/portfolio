The highlights

I'm based in Jönköping, Sweden, and have been working in sales for over a decade. My interest in technology has always been a significant part of my life, often using it to solve personal challenges. Recently, I've decided to further embrace this passion by pursuing studies in both front-end and back-end development after work hours. This decision stems from my desire to tackle even larger problems in the future.

Traveling the world with my partner is one of our shared dreams; both of us have a deep love for exploring new places and experiencing different cultures. When I'm not working or studying, I spend a considerable amount of time coding (sorry, Jessica 😁) and staying active, as I believe in the importance of maintaining physical health.

My eagerness to learn new things and reluctance to back down from a challenge define who I am. My journey into the world of programming is not just about career advancement but also about fulfilling a personal quest for knowledge and making a significant impact through technology.
